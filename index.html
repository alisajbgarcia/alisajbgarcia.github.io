Hi!

My name is Alisa.

I'm a junior at Purdue University majoring in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics. I have been coding since sophomore year in high school (~5 years) and have worked on projects in Java, Dart, Flutter, JavaScript, and more.

At Purdue, I am a part of several clubs which I really enjoy:

  • On BoilerMake, the student-run club that organizes Purdue's largest hackathon, I am on the Design team. I help out with creating the visual aspects of the hackathon and design flyers, website wireframes, and more. You can see examples of my work below!
  • On LaunchPad, Purdue's largest CS/DS mentorship community, I am a mentor. I am currently working on a project with a freshman CS student, which is a storytelling game.
  • I also play in Purdue Symphony Orchestra! I have been playing the viola for about 10 years and love listening to classical music in my free time. My favorite composers are Debussy, Tchaikovsky, and currently Rachmaninoff :).

Projects

National Kidney Foundation Diet app

I am a part of an EPICS team at Purdue that is working with the National Kidney Foundation to develop a diet-tracking app targeted to those with chronic kidney disease. Our aim is to help patients with CKD prevent the disease from advancing to more dangerous stages, which is all possible through diet management.

My roles: Software developer, project partner liaison

My Work:

  • I was in charge of the fluid tracking feature
  • I helped implement the food search bar
  • I worked on several authentication pages

This project uses:

  • Dart, Flutter, Firestore, NutrititonIX database
