Projects

Here are some of my favorite projects, a mix of programming and design.

National Kidney Foundation Diet app

I am a part of an EPICS team at Purdue that is working with the National Kidney Foundation to develop a diet-tracking app targeted to those with chronic kidney disease. Our aim is to help patients with CKD prevent the disease from advancing to more dangerous stages, which is all possible through diet management.

My roles: Software developer, project partner liaison

My Work:

  • I was in charge of the fluid tracking feature
  • I helped implement the food search bar
  • I worked on several authentication pages

This project uses:

  • Dart, Flutter, Firestore, NutrititonIX database

AthLink Website

I am on a team that is currently working on a web service called AthLink that helps athletes connect with each other. Users input sports they're interested in, location, availability for the next 36 hours, and their interest level. They are able to view potential matches of other atheletes who share similar traits, and will be able to message them within the app to coordinate workout plans.

This project uses:

  • Java, Angular, Spring, Firestore
